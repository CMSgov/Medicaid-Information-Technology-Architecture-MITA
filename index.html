Welcome to MITA and its Governance Board

The purpose of this repository is to introduce the Medicaid Information Technology Architecture (MITA) Framework to states and keep stakeholders abreast of the work being done by the MITA Governance Board (MGB).

MITA is a framework that establishes national guidelines for technologies, information, and processes that enable a standard approach to Informational Technology (IT) implementations for state Medicaid Enterprise Systems. MITA is included in sub-regulatory guidance to ensure state Medicaid IT projects align with national-level program goals and best practices. MITA 3.0 is the current version and MGB workgroup members are actively collaborating to develop an updated version, MITA 4.0.
